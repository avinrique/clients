About College - RV College of Engineering Bangalore (RVCE)

RV College of Engineering (RVCE) admits students to its B.E. and M.Tech programmes through multiple entrance channels. The admission process is designed to maintain merit, diversity, and inclusivity while aligning with AICTE and Government of Karnataka regulations.

Rashtreeya Vidyalaya (RV) College of Engineering Bangalore is one of the leading technical institutes located in the state of Karnataka. The campus is spread across approximately 43 acres near Mysore Road, Bengaluru with excellent road and public transport connectivity. The fee range is approximately INR 1,25,000 - 2,81,677 per annum depending on the programme and admission channel.

Entrance Exams Accepted by RVCE

RV College of Engineering takes admission through the Karnataka Common Entrance Test (K-CET) conducted by the Government of Karnataka through the Karnataka Examination Authority (KEA) or through the Consortium of Medical, Engineering and Dental Colleges of Karnataka (COMEDK) undergraduate test. Management quota admissions are based on academic merit and interview conducted by RVCE.

RVCE Fee Payment Guidelines

  1. Document verification by the college authorities.
  2. Fill DD with your name, mobile number, and branch details on the back.
  3. Submit the DD to the cashier at the college.
  4. Complete the online application form on the college portal.
  5. Print the application form with signatures and photographs.
  6. Collect the fee receipt from the cashier.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the most popular course at RVCE, Bangalore?
Engineering courses such as Computer Science, Mechanical, Civil, ECE, and Biotechnology are some of the most popular courses at RVCE, Bangalore.
What is the fee structure of RV College?
The KCET annual fees for B.E. is ₹1,25,000, COMEDK fees is ₹2,81,677. M.Tech fees is ₹1,20,000 per year. MCA is ₹1,20,000 per year, and MBA is ₹1,50,000 per year.
What is RV College of Engineering management quota fees?
The management quota fees vary by programme. For B.E. programmes, management quota fees are determined as per RVCE norms. For M.Tech, fees are approximately ₹1,20,000 per year. MCA and MBA fees follow the standard fee structure with management quota norms applied.
What are the COMEDK annual fees for B.E.?
The COMEDK annual fees for a B.E. program is ₹2,81,677 as tuition fee per year.
What is the KCET fee for B.E. at RVCE?
If you take admission through KCET, the tuition fees for B.E. is approximately ₹1,25,000 per year. Hostel and mess charges are additional and can be obtained from the college directly.
What are the top colleges from COMEDK?
The top colleges from COMEDK are: #1 R.V. College of Engineering, #2 PES Institute of Technology, #3 BMS College of Engineering, #4 M.S. Ramaiah Institute of Technology, and #5 Bangalore Institute of Technology.
What is the placement record at RVCE?
RVCE has an excellent placement record with 90-95% students placed annually. The highest package offered is ₹45 LPA (2024) and the average package ranges from ₹6-8 LPA. Top recruiters include Amazon, Microsoft, Google, Infosys, TCS, and Bosch.
How to apply for admission at RVCE?
Students can apply through KCET (45% seats), COMEDK-UGET (30% seats), or Management Quota (25% seats). For KCET and COMEDK, admission is through centralized counselling. For management quota, apply directly through RVCE based on academic merit and interview. Contact Vinayaka Consultancy for expert admission guidance.
